About MORE REAL GOODS

Our business was established with a clear mission: to give high-quality, wholesome, and natural food items. As a small business, we began by creating organic, gluten-free, and non-GMO items for health-conscious customers from our manufacturing site in Antipolo, Philippines. With the growing popularity of clean and sustainable food, we expanded our operations into manufacturing, tolling, and repacking, giving businesses and consumers access to high-quality raw materials. We began as a modest firm in Antipolo, Philippines, offering non-GMO, gluten-free, and organic products to health-conscious consumers.

Here at More Real Goods, we're dedicated to getting more nutritious organic, non-GMO, and natural food ingredients into the marketplace so that individuals can live a healthier life. We value integrity, honesty, and stewardship of the planet. We feel that the superior-quality nutrition has to be affordable, getting superior ingredients into families without compromising their health or principles.

We aim to be the world's greatest ethically sourced, quality food supplier so that future generations can be healthy and sustainable. We envision a world where nutritious food is not a luxury but a standard. Through innovation, responsible sourcing, and a fanatic focus on quality, we would like to disrupt the food business and leave a sustainable, long-term, positive footprint on the consumer and the planet.

Our Story

What began as a simple but effective goal to deliver nutritious, organic, and sustainable food to the masses has evolved into a well known manufacturing facility. Through collaborations with local and global suppliers, we established a solid base, where our customers get nothing but the highest quality products. Nowadays, More Real Goods is known for supplying superior raw materials and dependable food processing technologies.

Our Values

  • Quality: We believe the best ingredients are where great food starts. Every product is put through a comprehensive quality testing process to ensure that it satisfies the highest requirements for nutrition, taste, and purity.
  • Safety: Your safety comes first. Therefore, we follow strict safety guidelines and international best practices to make sure that each product is treated, processed, and packed with care, so you can trust what you eat.
  • Sustainability: Great meals should not cost the earth. We are devoted to sustainable living, from responsible ingredient sourcing to decreasing packaging waste and lowering our overall environmental effect.
  • Innovation: We're always looking for new ways to improve. By means of research, new technologies, or strategic partnerships, we lead the industry trends to make our products and processes better, delivering the best in natural and sustainable food to you.

Our Facility

Located in Antipolo, Philippines, our facility is constructed according to the highest standards of food quality and safety. With the latest processing equipment, tolling facilities, and repacking, we guarantee every product meets regulations tightly and surpasses the expectations of customers.

Community Involvement

We are committed to giving back to the communities we serve. More Real Goods actively supports local farmers, food banks, and sustainability initiatives. Through local partnerships, we support health and nutrition programs, making sure more people have access to healthy food.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

Sustainability is at the heart of what we do. Our initiatives include:

  • Ethical sourcing of raw materials
  • Eco-friendly, minimal-waste packaging
  • Energy-efficient production to reduce carbon footprint
  • Supporting local sustainability projects
